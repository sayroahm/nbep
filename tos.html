Terms of Service & User Agreement  |  Effective: 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing, browsing, or using the NBEP website (nbep-us.com) and any affiliated services, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service ("TOS"). If you do not agree to these terms, you must immediately terminate your purpose of use of this site.

2. Nature of the Organization

The National Bureau of Exploitation Protection (NBEP) is a non-government, non-profit organization. NBEP is not a law enforcement agency. We serve as a reporting intermediary to assist in escalating potential cyber crime cases to appropriate authorities. In any emergency, contact 911 or your local law enforcement immediately.

3. Prohibited Conduct

Users are strictly prohibited from engaging in any of the following while using NBEP services:

  • Submitting false, fraudulent, or misleading tips or reports
  • Attempting to gain unauthorized access to NBEP internal systems or administrative services
  • Harassment, intimidation, or threats directed at NBEP staff, volunteers, or associated parties
  • Using NBEP platforms to facilitate or promote criminal activity of any kind
  • Automated scraping, crawling, or data harvesting of any NBEP site content
  • Impersonating NBEP officials, directors, or volunteers

4. Privacy Policy

NBEP does not collect, log, or store any personally identifiable information including IP addresses, browser cookies, or tracking identifiers unless explicitly stated and consented to. Submitted tip data is handled in accordance with applicable law and handled with strict confidentiality protocols.

5. Enforcement & Penalties

Failure to comply with the provided TOS will result in a permanent IP ban and a "No-Affiliation" policy with your specific user. Severe violations including fraudulent reporting or targeted attacks on NBEP infrastructure may be reported to appropriate federal or state authorities.

6. Volunteer Agreement

Volunteers who are accepted into NBEP roles agree to additional conduct expectations outlined in the Volunteer Onboarding Agreement. Volunteers may not represent NBEP in any official capacity without explicit written authorization from the Director.

Access to NBEP-distributed software is granted on a case-by-case basis. Unauthorized distribution, reverse engineering, or modification of NBEP software is strictly prohibited. Software access may be revoked at any time without notice.
