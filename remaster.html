The NBEP Objective

The National Bureau of Exploitation Protection (NBEP) operates as a sophisticated bridge between digital intelligence and law enforcement action. Our primary goal is to scour the depths of the internet to identify illicit content and the perpetrators behind it, ensuring that evidence is preserved and delivered to the correct federal and local agencies.

Digital Surveillance

Our volunteer specialists utilize advanced digital forensics and monitoring techniques to locate exploitation networks that often evade standard automated filters. We focus on high-impact targets and systemic exploitation rings.

Evidence Preservation

Reporting is only effective if it is actionable. NBEP ensures that all discovered content is metadata-logged and timestamped according to standards that assist federal investigators in building a robust legal case.

Federal Liaison

We do not take the law into our own hands. Once a threat is verified, the NBEP intelligence package is securely transmitted to the FBI, IC3, and relevant local jurisdictions for immediate intervention.

Join the Coalition

The NBEP does not officially hire employees, nor are we a government-funded entity. We are a collective of dedicated citizens. You simply volunteer your time and expertise for our vigilante group to protect the innocent.
